Detailed Description

Used to render and load textures. Includes the ability to resize, scale, flip, recolour etc. 2D games are made up of these.

◆ FlipFlags

enum ASGE::Sprite::FlipFlags

Used to flip the texture's UV coordinates. The flip is performed inside the shader and on the CPU.

Enumerator
NORMAL 

texture is not flipped.

FLIP_X 

texture is flipped on the x axis.

FLIP_Y 

texture is flipped on the y axis.

FLIP_BOTH 

texture is flipped on both axis.
